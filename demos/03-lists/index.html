How to make Honey Muffins
  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder in a small bowl
  2. Mix eggs, honey, oat milk, oat milk and vanilla
  3. Add the dry mixture to the liquid one in two seperate batches mixing thoroughly
  4. Distribute mixture into muffin tins
  5. Bake for 16 minutes or until lightly browned
  6. Enjoy!
